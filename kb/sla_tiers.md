# Service Level Agreements by Tier

## Overview

This document defines the service level agreements (SLAs) for each account tier.

## SLA Summary

| Tier | Uptime SLA | Response Time | Resolution Target |
|------|------------|---------------|-------------------|
| Enterprise | 99.99% | 1 hour | 4 hours |
| Professional | 99.9% | 4 hours | 24 hours |
| Starter | 99.5% | 24 hours | 72 hours |
| Free | Best effort | Best effort | Best effort |

## Enterprise Tier

### Uptime Commitment

- 99.99% monthly uptime guarantee
- Maximum 4.32 minutes downtime per month
- Excludes scheduled maintenance windows

### Support Response Times

| Priority | Initial Response | Update Frequency | Resolution Target |
|----------|------------------|------------------|-------------------|
| P0 | 15 minutes | 30 minutes | 4 hours |
| P1 | 1 hour | 2 hours | 8 hours |
| P2 | 4 hours | Daily | 48 hours |
| P3 | 24 hours | Weekly | 1 week |

### Additional Benefits

- Dedicated account manager
- Direct Slack channel with engineering
- Quarterly business reviews
- Custom integration support
- 24/7 phone support for P0/P1

### Service Credits

| Uptime | Credit |
|--------|--------|
| 99.9% - 99.99% | 10% |
| 99.0% - 99.9% | 25% |
| 95.0% - 99.0% | 50% |
| Below 95.0% | 100% |

## Free Tier

### Service Level

- Best effort uptime
- No SLA guarantees
- Community support only
- May experience throttling during high load

### Limitations

- No guaranteed response times
- No service credits
- Limited to community forum support
- Features may be restricted

## Maintenance Windows

### Scheduled Maintenance

- Announced 7 days in advance
- Typically Sunday 2am-6am PT
- Enterprise customers can request alternative windows
- Emergency maintenance may occur with shorter notice

### Maintenance Exclusions

Scheduled maintenance windows do not count against uptime SLAs when:
- Announced at least 72 hours in advance
- Duration is under 4 hours
- Occurs during designated maintenance window

## Requesting SLA Credits

### How to Request

1. Submit request within 30 days of incident
2. Include incident date and duration
3. Reference any incident numbers
4. Credits applied to next invoice

### Credit Limitations

- Maximum credit: 100% of monthly fee
- Credits are not transferable
- Credits expire after 12 months
- Cannot be exchanged for cash
