# Billing Policies

## Overview

This document outlines our billing policies, payment terms, and dispute resolution procedures.

## Payment Terms

### Invoice Schedule

- Enterprise accounts are billed monthly in arrears
- Professional accounts are billed monthly in advance
- Starter accounts are billed monthly in advance
- All invoices are due within 30 days of issue date

### Accepted Payment Methods

- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- ACH bank transfer (US accounts only)
- Wire transfer (Enterprise accounts only)
- Purchase orders accepted for Enterprise annual contracts

## Pricing Tiers

### Enterprise Tier

- Custom pricing based on usage and contract terms
- Volume discounts available
- Dedicated account manager included
- Annual contracts with quarterly true-ups

### Professional Tier

- $499/month base fee
- Usage-based charges for API calls over 100,000/month
- $0.001 per additional API call
- Monthly billing cycle

### Starter Tier

- $49/month flat fee
- Includes up to 10,000 API calls
- $0.005 per additional API call
- No long-term commitment required

## Overage Charges

### How Overages Work

Overage charges apply when usage exceeds the included allocation for your tier. Overages are calculated at the end of each billing cycle and added to the following month's invoice.

### Overage Notifications

- Email notification at 80% of allocation
- Email notification at 100% of allocation
- Dashboard warning displayed when approaching limits

## Dispute Resolution

### Filing a Dispute

To dispute a charge:
1. Contact billing support within 60 days of the charge
2. Provide invoice number and specific line items in question
3. Include any supporting documentation

### Review Process

- All disputes are reviewed within 5 business days
- Customer will receive written response with findings
- If error confirmed, credit issued within 1 billing cycle

### Refund Policy

- Refunds issued for billing errors within 90 days
- Service credits preferred for active accounts
- Refunds processed to original payment method
- Processing time: 5-10 business days

## Account Cancellation

### Cancellation Terms

- 30-day notice required for Enterprise accounts
- Professional and Starter accounts can cancel anytime
- No refunds for partial months
- Data retained for 30 days after cancellation

### Final Invoice

- Final invoice includes all usage through cancellation date
- Any outstanding balance due within 30 days
- Credits applied to final invoice automatically
